About

Welcome to our website, created an engineering graduate who are passionate about the world of cryptocurrency. Our platform offers a unique solution for predicting cryptocurrency prices using LSTM Neural Networks. We understand the importance of accurate and reliable predictions in the volatile cryptocurrency market.

That's why we have implemented our code in such a way that users can easily enter the cryptocurrency coin code and currency code of their choice to get a prediction graph generated by our LSTM model.
To further assist users in making informed decisions, we also have a suggestion tab where they can fill out a survey to get recommendations on the best coins to invest in.
Additionally, we have provided links for users to gain deeper knowledge in this domain. We hope our website will be a valuable resource for cryptocurrency enthusiasts and investors alike.
